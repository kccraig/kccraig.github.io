My Target Audience

The target audience of my site will be a varied group.

I hope to appeal to the "weird" group of people looking for more information about Idaho. They like to travel to far away places.

The local audience will enjoy up to date weather information in a more entertaining format.

Kids will enjoy my site because I'll include fun facts about the local area.

Anyone searching Idaho weather will be able to find easy access here.
