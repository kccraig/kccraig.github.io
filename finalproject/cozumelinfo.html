Fun Things to do in Cozumel

Shopping Fun for Everyone

Shopping in Cozumel is amazing! From silver to shoes, gold to art galleries, you will find the best shopping at the most desirable prices. Come in out of the sun to find treasures from the Island of the Swallows.

San Gervasio Archaeological Zone

Built one thousand years ago, these ancient ruins are what is left of the pre-Columbian Maya civilization. The shrine was built to the Mayan goddess of fertility Ixchel. Tour the ruins on your own or be smart and take a guided tour to learn all you can about this ancient Mayan wonder.

Snorkeling in Cozumel

Dotted with coral reefs, Cozumel is a destination paradise for snorkeling. The wonders of the ocean await you under the sea. Arrange for diving expeditions before you go for the best prices. Snorkels and Fins snorkeling company gives cruise passenger discounts.
