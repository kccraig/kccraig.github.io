Design Principles

Kathleen Clark Craig

White Space

Exxon Mobile Company

www.exxon.com

Exxon uses white space around all elements in order to keep their website clean and easy to use.

Fitt's Law

Harvest Hope Food Bank

www.harvesthope.org

Fitt's Law is well illustrated by the placement of the donate button that is placed directly aligned to the right of the centered logo and header of the company. The donate button is the second element you see when you open the page.

Repetition

Red

ww.red.org

Red.org uses the obvious principle of repetition of the color red throughout the website.
