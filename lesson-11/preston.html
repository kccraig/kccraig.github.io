Events This Year in Preston

The Rodeo is Coming!

That Famous Preston Night Rodeo is this month!! This year marks the 156th year since the first reputed rodeo. Cowboys have been steering off against each other for a long time in the fight for best roper. Preston takes great pride in its world famous rodeo. Events this year include Mutton Bustin' for the little ones 6 and under. All await the crowning of Rodeo Queen and Junior Rodeo Queen on Friday night. Bull riding starts at 8:30 pm. Be in your seats early to watch the cowboys lose theirs! Come and watch all the roping and riding events this year at the 86th annual That Famous Preston Night Rodeo!
